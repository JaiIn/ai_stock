## Candidate Input Contract Summary

The contract accepts only caller-supplied candidate inputs and returns
deterministic validation results and an aggregate preflight summary. It is a
pure no-I/O contract layer and does not load, persist, rank, recommend, trade,
or display candidates.

## Validation Policy Summary

- Symbol is trimmed.
- Empty symbol is invalid.
- Unsafe or too-long symbol is invalid.
- Unknown market normalizes to `UNKNOWN`.
- Forbidden or unknown source becomes `unsupported_source`.
- Disabled candidate becomes `disabled_candidate`.
- Later duplicates become `duplicate_candidate`.
- Data shortage becomes a safe `insufficient_data` or `needs_review` status.

## Duplicate Handling Policy

Duplicates are marked deterministically by normalized symbol within the
caller-supplied batch. The policy only reports `duplicate_candidate`; it does
not store, merge, or rewrite watchlists.

## Insufficient Data Handling Policy

Insufficient or partial data is not treated as advice. It is reported as a
safe status so a later stage can display or review it without producing a
recommendation or action directive.
